5 Unmissable Red Flags for Fake Drinks

  • The Seal Check: Authentic bottles have clear, tamper-proof seals with specific holographic branding that doesn't just peel off.
  • Label Precision: Look for misspellings or blurry printing. Premium brands use high-quality paper and often have embossed text.
  • The Bubble Test: When you shake a bottle of spirit, the bubbles should disappear quickly. If they linger, it might be due to added sugar or low-quality additives.
  • Smell & Taste: If you detect a strong chemical or acetone smell that doesn't belong, stop immediately. Genuine whisky has a layered aroma.
  • Price Logic: If a bottle of Blue Label is being sold for KES 5,000, it is 100% fake. Avoid "back-of-the-car" sellers.
